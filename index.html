Party in style

Kick your celebration up a notch!


Book Your Party

The Quickie $250

Would you like to celebrate with DivaFit but are pressed for time? The Quickie is perfect for those who want to have fun and learn a few moves in the process. This one hour party can be either pole or chair themed and is a great way to see what DivaFit is all about! Grab a few friends and learn the basic moves in this fast moving hour!

The Diva $300

Party like a Diva in our signature party that is an hour and a half of pure fun! You will learn a sexy chair strip-tease routine, pole dance moves, and a spin. Want to add to the sexy fun? Bring a button down shirt to play with in the chair routine. Don’t forget your cameras and your friends because this is a party worth remembering!

The Night Out $350

Throw the ultimate Diva party with our two hour Night Out package! It starts with 30 minutes of social time before the exhilarating dancing begins. During the hour and a half dance time you will learn chair/striptease routines, pole dance moves and a spin. Bring food and non-alcoholic beverages to complete the fun!
